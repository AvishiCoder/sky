Sky





Sky was a golden retriever. He came to our house on 4 september, 2020. He was 1 month old when he came. He was a foodie. When we came back home after school, he came running from anywhere he would be when we took his name. We taught him to sit and stay. Then at 24 November, 2020, he died because of stomach obstruction. And because of stomach obstruction, doctor said to give nothing to him to eat, And he was foodie so we still cry that we couldn't feed him in his last days. So now, on 24 November each year, we feed food to dogs roaming in street and also keep food at the place he was buried.
